Enchanted Rose

This app controls the Enchanted Rose prop via Bluetooth.

Tips

  1. Make sure batteries are fresh!
  2. On your phone, double-check that Bluetooth is on
  3. Reset the petals to their leftmost position and then check CAREFULLY under the prop to ensure the wires are all connected and go through the stem up to the flower.
  4. The petals should just have enough of a grip on the wires to hang on. The wires only move about a half inch at most when the petals are dropped.
  5. The prop has three power sources: one for the Arduino board, one for the servo motors and one for the lights. Double-check that they are all connected.
  6. If app doesn't show "Ready", verify prop is all turned on, then close this app and re-launch.
  7. Slide the slider controls to drop the petals.
