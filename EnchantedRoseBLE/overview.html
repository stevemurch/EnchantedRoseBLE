Enchanted Rose

This app controls the Enchanted Rose prop via Bluetooth.

Tips

  1. Make sure batteries are fresh!
  2. Turn on Servo Switch, Accent Light Switch, and plug in Arduino. Then, launch the app.
  3. On your phone, double-check that Bluetooth is on
  4. The prop has three power sources: one for the Arduino board, one for the servo motors and one for the lights. Double-check that they are all connected.
  5. If the app background isn't in color, the app cannot reach the prop. Verify all three prop switches are flipped UP (on), then close this app and re-launch.

For more information, please visit this How To Guide.
