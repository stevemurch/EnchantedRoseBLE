Enchanted Rose

Tips

  1. Make sure batteries are fresh!
  2. Turn on Bluetooth on your phone.
  3. Make sure the prop's circuit board is powered AND the servo motors are powered.
  4. Reset the petals to their leftmost position and then check CAREFULLY under the prop to ensure the wires are all connected and go through the stem up to the flower.
  5. The petals should just have enough of a grip on the wires to hang on. The wires only move about a half inch at most when the petals are dropped.
  6. If app doesn't show "Ready", verify prop is all turned on, then close this app and re-launch.
  7. Slide the slider controls to drop the petals.
